Who We Are

For over twenty years, PT. Indo Furnitama Raya (IFURA) has consistently delivered outstanding woodworking product with excellent performance benefiting our customers, our investors, our employees, our partners and the communities as well as the forest in which we operate.

Wood has been our living and growing source, wood is a commodity as well as an experience, a testament of time, with details and characteristics, stimulating our senses, and giving us a sense of quality of life. We respect wood as we respect nature, therefore we continue to promote responsible wood manufacturing to balance the nature.

Top Quality - From Sourcing to Finishing

Wood is a material we treat with the utmost respect, and wood deserves to age with dignity. That’s why we put our biggest investment at quality sourcing, with the legal wood harvesting license and the formal forest management rights from Indonesian government we are able to control all the little things that matters from selecting the best material until finishing. IFURA is turning legal wood into a strong, sustainable and long lasting part of our future. Like nature itself, IFURA’s wood requires minimum maintenance and aged to be more beautiful every year.
